Rubz Restaurant was founded with a passion for exquisite Filipino cuisine and impeccable service. Our story began with a simple imagination and turning it into reality. Our vision is to create a restaurant that serves both classic Filipino comfort food and innovative twists on beloved dishes. We wanted Rubz Restaurant to be a place where people could experience the heart of Filipino cuisine while also introducing modern elements that cater to contemporary tastes.


Rubz, our mission is to bring the diverse and vibrant flavors of Filipino cuisine to the world, blending tradition with innovation. We are dedicated to preserving the authenticity of Filipino culinary heritage while embracing contemporary techniques and local ingredients to provide a unique dining experience. We aim to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where guests can explore the richness of Filipino culture through food and share memorable moments with family and friends.


We strive to stay true to the classic flavors of Filipino food while continually experimenting with new techniques and ideas to create exciting and unique dishes that appeal to modern tastes. We want to honor the traditions of Filipino cooking while also pushing the boundaries of what Filipino food can be.
